Who are we?

At 180math, we strongly believe every child can succeed in math when they are taught with patience, clarity, and encouragement. Our mission is to create a supportive learning environment where students feel comfortable asking questions, building confidence, and mastering concepts at their own pace.


Founded in 2026, 180math.com is the result of more than a decade of teaching experience led by Jyothi, whose passion for helping students has positively impacted learners from elementary school through high school. Known for her warm, approachable teaching style, she has a unique ability to break down complex topics into simple, easy-to-understand steps that make math less intimidating and more enjoyable.


What sets us apart is our focus on small class sizes allowing every student to receive personal attention and actively participate in understanding the concepts. We know that children learn best when they feel seen, heard, and supported; Our classrooms are designed to do exactly that. Whether in person or online, we combine personalized instruction, structured learning, and a friendly atmosphere to help students strengthen their skills, improve performance, and develop a lasting confidence in math.


Math is about more than just solving problems. It’s about building confidence, curiosity, and resilience. Our mission is to empower students to believe in their own potential as strongly as we believe in them, turning the 'intimidation' often associated with math into a sense of achievement, enjoyment, and discovery.
