Frequently Asked Questions

How big is the class size in a group cohort?

There would be a maximum of 6 Students in each batch. Small batches help the students stay focused and get individualized attention.

Do you offer homework help?

Challenging questions are always welcome in the class and benefit all students. However, the students are expected to try solving their homework at home before requesting help in the class. Their attempted work, even if wrong, identifies the exact concept they need coaching on.

Do you assign homework?

Yes, homework is assigned weekly, either via textbook or Deltamath. Students are also encouraged to keep practicing multiplication tables 1 through 20 every week to help speed up their mental calculations. Parents are accountable to make sure their child completes the work on time and satisfactorily. A separate book for homework is requested.

What is your pricing?

We offer comprehensive tiers for both Private and Group cohorts. Click here to view our detailed pricing page.

When are the fees due?

Fees are expected to be paid by the 5th of every month.

Are the fees prorated if my child is absent for a session?

No, fees will not be prorated as the class still commenced for other students. The teacher will share with the student what was covered in the class in the form of whiteboard material, topics, or instructional videos. The student will have extra help during the next class as well.

Are the fees prorated if the teacher cancels a session?

Standard monthly fees have been calculated considering 2 emergency cancellations by the teacher through the academic year. If there are more cancellations for any reason, every effort will be made for a makeup class. If that is not possible, fees will be prorated in the following month.

School Calendar indicates 42 or 43 weeks depending on the last day of the school. Why are the kids only receiving 36 weeks of lessons?

4 Holiday weeks*** and 2 emergency cancellations have been excluded from the 42 academic weeks.

Other than the 4 weeks mentioned, do students get additional holidays?

Currently, we follow the South Windsor Public School calendar. For Holidays falling on a weekday, such as Diwali, Holi, Presidents Day etc., parents will be consulted if a Holiday is needed. We will go with the majority. Fees will be prorated in the following month if the class is cancelled.

* Extra lessons are charged at Private lesson rate.

** No classes Thanksgiving week and Christmas Holidays.

*** Holiday weeks with no lessons:

  • Thanksgiving week: November 23rd to November 27th 2027
  • Christmas Holidays: December 21st, 2026 to January 1st, 2027
  • Spring Break: April 12th - April 16th 2027
