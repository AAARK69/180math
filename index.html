How big is the class size in a group cohort?

There would be a maximum of 6 Students in each batch. Small batches help the students stay focussed and get individualized attention.

Do you offer homework help?

Challenging questions are always welcome in the class and benefit all students. However, students are expected to try solving their homework at home before requesting help in the class. Their attempted work, even if wrong, identifies the exact concept they need coaching on.

Do you assign homework?

Yes, homework is assigned weekly, either via textbook problems or Deltamath. Students are also encouraged to keep practicing multiplication tables 1 through 20 every week to help speed up their mental calculations. Parents are accountable to make sure their child completes the work on time and satisfactorily. A separate book for homework is requested.

When are the fees due?

Fees are expected to be paid by 5th of every month.

Will fees be prorated for the month of June since it has only two working weeks?

Yes. The June fees will be prorated, and the adjustment will be applied in April 2027, based on the official last day of the school year.

Are the fees prorated if my child is absent for a session?

No, fees are not prorated for student absences, as the class is still conducted for the rest of the group. If a student misses a session, the teacher will provide all materials covered—such as whiteboard notes, topics reviewed, and instructional videos. The student will also receive additional support in the following class to help them catch up.

Are the fees prorated if the teacher cancels a session?

Standard monthly fees are calculated with the understanding that up to two emergency teacher cancellations may occur during the academic year. If additional cancellations happen for any reason, every effort will be made to schedule a makeup class. If a makeup session cannot be arranged, the fees will be prorated in the following month.

The school calendar shows 42–43 weeks. Why are students receiving 36 weeks of lessons?

The school year includes 42 academic weeks, but 4 holiday weeks*** and 2 emergency teacher-cancellation weeks are already accounted for in the annual schedule. After excluding these six weeks, students receive 36 weeks of instruction.

If the school board extends the academic year by an additional week, we will be notified around April 2027, and class schedules will be extended accordingly.

Other than the 4 weeks mentioned, do students get additional holidays?

Currently, we follow the South Windsor Public School calendar. For Holidays falling on a weekday, such as Diwali, Holi, Presidents Day etc., parents will be consulted if a Holiday is needed. We will go with the majority. Fees will be prorated in the following month if the class is cancelled on a holiday.
